Protosite brainstorm ideas

Jabberwocky website

Jabberwocky is a student-run literary journal affiliated with the UMass English department (though any UMass student can submit writing). They do not currently have a website (just a mention on the UMass English Dept. website), so this would allow our editorial board to post digital versions of past issues, call for submissions, list contact info, and so on.

First-time Home Buyers website

This would be an informational website for first-time home buyers. I am interested in this topic and would like a chance to do further research. The core sections of the website would probably follow the general process of home-buying: what can you afford? (financing/loans), shopping for homes, making an offer, home inspections, and closing the sale.
